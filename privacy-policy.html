Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Last updated July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you use the Euchre with Friends website and app, including app features that use advertising and in-app purchases.

Information You Provide

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and any other information you include in your message.

Platform Services

Euchre with Friends may rely on Apple services such as the App Store, Game Center, and iCloud for downloads, achievements, leaderboards, and live table features. Those services may process information according to Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Advertising

Euchre with Friends may show ads through Google AdMob, including rewarded ads that let you earn in-app coins and interstitial ads shown at natural breaks such as after a game. Google and its advertising partners may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers when permitted, IP address, app and ad interactions, crash or diagnostic data, and other information needed to deliver, measure, limit, and improve ads.

On iOS, the app may ask for permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before using information to show more relevant ads. You can change tracking permission at any time in iOS Settings. If you decline tracking, ads may still appear, but they may be less personalized.

You can learn more about how Google uses information for advertising at Google's advertising privacy page.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Euchre with Friends may offer in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit and the App Store, including coin packs, ad-free or unlimited-play access, subscription plans, and other digital items. Apple processes App Store payments and related account information. We do not receive your credit card number or other full payment details.

The app may receive and store purchase-related information needed to provide what you bought, such as product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase status, entitlement status, restore status, token balances, and local transaction history. This information is used to unlock purchased content, restore purchases, grant coins or bonuses, determine whether ads should be shown, and provide support.

You can manage subscriptions and purchase settings through your Apple ID and App Store account. You can learn more about Apple's App Store privacy practices at Apple's App Store privacy page.

Diagnostics and Analytics

Crash reports, diagnostics, and similar technical information may be made available by platform providers to help improve app stability, performance, and gameplay quality.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to operate the app, respond to support requests, maintain achievements and leaderboard features, provide ads and rewards, process purchases and subscriptions, restore entitlements, prevent abuse, and improve the quality of the experience.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the app, provide platform services, deliver or measure ads, process purchases, respond to support requests, or comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can control App Tracking Transparency permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage subscriptions, purchase history, and payment settings through your Apple ID and App Store account.
  • You can contact us with privacy questions or support requests using the email below.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
